Student Affairs Graduate Assistantship Recruitment Program-This site will provide you with information about Graduate Assistantships available in the Division of Student Affairs.

Funding for Graduate School can be a challenge for many students. East Carolina University provides many different sources to help defray the cost of graduate school education. Here are some resources for your consideration:

Departmental Graduate Assistantships- the Department of Recreation and Leisure Studies has Graduate Assistantships that are offered to the most qualified applicants. Interested students are encourage to complete the Graduate Assistantship application and submit to the Graduate Program Director as early as possible. Assistantships are provided to the most qualified candidates who have been accepted as a graduate student at East Carolina University.

ECU Campus Recreation & Wellness (CRW) Graduate Assistantships- ECU Campus Recreation & Wellness hire Graduate Assistants to assist with campus recreation programming. Many RCLS graduate students in the past have been successful in being hired in these positions. CRW typically hires Graduate Assistants in Intramural Sports & Family Programs, Campus Wellness, Physical Activity & Fitness, Marketing, Adventure/Leadership, and Club Sports. These positions typically require some experience in the chosen program area and can also serve as valuable practical experience for recreation professionals. Applications should include a cover letter, resume, and list of at least three references and should be emailed directly to CRWGAS@ecu.edu.

College of Health & Human Performance Scholarships- The College of Health & Human Performance offers scholarships that are available to any major within the college, as well as scholarships that are specific to individual departments and majors within the College of Health & Human Performance.

Graduate School Financial Resources - the ECU Graduate School provides a listing of financial resources to help make your graduate education affordable and attainable.

Student Scholarships- ECUAWard is East Carolina University's on-line scholarship application portal. Completion of the General Scholarship Application will allow interested students to access a variety of scholarship opportunities on our campus.
